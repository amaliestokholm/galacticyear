# Showcase: What is a Galactic year

This is a small visualisation that puts the time scales of Galactic phenomenae into the context of Earth's history.

One tricky thing to understand when diving into Galactic archaeology (and astronomy in general) are the time scales. Stars, galaxies, the universe -- they all operate on time scales much longer than the human time scales. Here I am putting different events that has happened on Earth into the time scale of the movement of the Sun and solar system in our galaxy, the Milky Way.

This is deeply inspired by a slide I normally use in outreach talks, but I have expanded upon it here.

This will tie neatly into a follow-up piece of explanation on Galactic Archaeology coming soon.
